Timestamp Microservice

Example Usage:

  • [project url]/api/timestamp/2015-12-25
  • [project url]/api/timestamp/1451001600000

You can pass in a time in either format and receive back a JSON-formatted response with your time in UNIX time or UTC String.

Example Output:

{"unix":1451001600000, "utc":"Fri, 25 Dec 2015 00:00:00 GMT"}

Original concept forked from boilerplate at freeCodeCamp.org
